Educational Institutional Constructions


BT ’s experience in the education arena dates back to the 1950s and has continued to be one of our key areas of interest and expertise. Working in conjunction with our clients, we ensure that our buildings help provide a learning environment that is unparalleled in preparing students academically and personally for higher education and for the challenges of thinking independently, respecting individuality and acting with integrity.

“The leadership, knowledge and experience BT Constructors brings to the construction process is immense. Not only do they provide great craftsmanship during construction, but it is done with the utmost integrity.” Tim Abrams, Henry County Public Schools
Complete demolition and regarding of the schools existing football field. New construction included a new 1,900 seat stadium, 8 lane running track and all necessary track and field event stations. Also included new lighting upgrades, restrooms and concession facilities.

BT Constructors was tasked to renovate 20,000 sq. ft. and build a new 35,000 sq. ft. addition to the existing Henry County High School and keep school operations intact and minimize disruptions during construction. The addition included a new state of the art kitchen/cafeteria, new practice gym, corridor with concessions for existing gymnasium, all new administration offices, new agricultural classroom, new media center, several new classrooms, and 400 seat auditorium. Renovation of the existing school including new floors, ceilings, paint and double tiered lockers was completed during summer break. The site was renovated adding several needed parking slots, new parent and bus drop off, and traffic flow pattern. This site work was completed over the summer months to avoid disruption to the schools daily activities.
